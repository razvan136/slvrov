About

We're a team based in Felton, CA with students from San Lorenzo Middle and High schools. We compete in the MATE ROV Competition, with our regional location as Watsonville High School.

Vision

The MATE ROV Competition is an underwater robotics challenge that engages a global community of learners each year. We're a team based in Felton, CA with students from San Lorenzo Middle and High schools.
